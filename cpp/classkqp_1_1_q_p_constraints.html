template<typename Scalar>
class kqp::QPConstraints< Scalar >

Multiplying with matrix G

Real case: 

 -G1          -Id
    ...       -Id
         -Gr  -Id
 G1           -Id
    ...       -Id
          Gr  -Id

where Gr is \( nu_i * Id_n \) (real case) or \( nu_i * [ Id_n, Id_n; Id_n -Id_n] \) of size 2nr x n (r+1)
